```mermaid
    Process->>CLI: Command::new(claude).spawn()

    par 三线程
        Process->>Process: Waiter Task (进程生命周期)
        CLI-->>Process: stdout NDJSON lines
        Process->>Protocol: StreamLine::parse(line)
        Protocol->>Protocol: to_frontend_event(session_id)
        Protocol->>Tauri: emit("stream-event", event)
        Tauri->>Vue: useStreamProcessor 监听
        Vue->>Vue: chat store 更新 → re-render
    and
        CLI-->>Process: stderr lines
        Process->>Tauri: emit("stream-error", line)
    end

    CLI-->>Process: 进程退出
    Process->>Tauri: emit("process-exited", event)
    Process->>DB: set_session_status("completed")
```
